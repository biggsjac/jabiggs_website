Current Research


Since 2022, I have been fortunate enough to become involved in two different, yet equally intriguing, projects. Both these projects are centered in Belize where the majority of my previous research and experience took place.

First, I joined the Belize River East Archaeology (BREA) Project for their summer 2022 field season as their project osteologist/bioarchaeologsist. The eastern watershed of the Belize River is immensely complex, yet understudied compared to many other pertions of the Maya Lowlands. This project, under the direction of Dr. Ellie Harrison-Buck from the University of New Hampshire, is aimed at understanding this fascinating area from the Historic Period to the Archaic. As BREA's bioarchaeologist, I have begun formally analyzing the human biological material from past excavations, as well as conducted excavations of my own.
